The Orange Blossom Eatery

Blooming with Flavor

The Orange Blossom Eatery brings the vibrant flavors of Florida to your table. Nestled in the heart of Orlando, our restaurant is where the sunny climate and fresh ingredients inspire every meal. Whether it's a leisurely weekend brunch, a delightful lunch, or an intimate dinner, we create a dining experience that embodies the warmth and joy of the Sunshine State.

Menu Highlights

Brunch: Classic Eggs Benedict, Mimosa Pancakes, Fresh Fruit Bowls.

Lunch: Citrus Grilled Chicken Salad, Cuban Sandwiches, Sweet Potato Fries.

Dinner: Fresh Catch of the Day, Floridian Paella, Key Lime Pie for dessert.
